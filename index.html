Why It Works

Built around how memory actually works

Active recall, not passive tapping

Every lesson requires you to construct the answer yourself. Research consistently shows active recall produces retention rates two to three times higher than recognition-based learning.

10 minutes a day

Lessons are designed for focused 10-minute sessions. Short enough to fit into any schedule, structured enough that each session builds meaningfully on the last.

Visible, measurable progress

Streaks, XP, and lesson completion screens give you a clear picture of how far you have come. Progress you can see is progress that motivates you to continue.

German only, done right

Unlike multi-language platforms, every feature in DeutschGenie is built specifically for German. The compound word mechanic, the case drills, the sentence structure — all designed for one language.

Mobile-first design

Designed from the ground up for smartphone screens. No stripped-down mobile version — the phone is the primary experience, optimised for commutes, lunch breaks, and spare moments.

Compete on the leaderboard

Every lesson earns XP that puts you on a global leaderboard. Seeing your name climb past other learners adds a layer of motivation that solo practice simply cannot replicate.
